# Product Context: Parkshare

## User Experience ("Why" and "What")
- **Problem**: Inefficient use of allocated parking spots when owners are out of office or remote.
- **Solution**: A platform that facilitates the temporary transfer of parking rights within the team.
- **Visual Style**: High-end, "Orendt Studios" aesthetic. Minimalist, high contrast (Black/White), neon yellow accent (#E8FF00).
- **Target Audience**: Employees of Orendt Studios.
